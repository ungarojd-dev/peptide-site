Vendor Guide

Simple instructions for getting your products listed and keeping pricing current.

How It Works

1

Initial Setup, One Time

Send us your full product catalog using the format below. We'll have you live within 2 business days.

2

Weekly Updates, Every Monday

Email your updated price sheet each Monday. We update the site the same day when possible. If there is no update, we carry forward the last verified pricing.

3

Product Changes, As Needed

Adding or removing a product? Update your sheet and flag it in your email. We'll handle the rest.

Product Sheet Format

Send your sheet as a CSV, Excel file, or Google Sheets link. Use exactly these 7 columns in this order.

SKU Product Name Compound Form Mg / Unit Price ($) In Stock
BPC-5MG BPC-157 5mg Vial BPC-157 Lyophilized 5 39.99 Yes
TB500-10MG TB-500 10mg Vial TB-500 Lyophilized 10 59.99 No
A few rules: SKU, no spaces, hyphens only  ·  Price, plain number, no $ sign  ·  In Stock, Yes or No only  ·  Form, use: Lyophilized, Nasal Spray, Oral, Cream, or Tablet

Weekly Email Format

Use this format every Monday. Attach your updated sheet or paste a Google Sheets link.

Subject: [Your Company], Weekly Pricing Update [Date] Hi team,
Attached is our updated sheet for the week of [Date].
Changes: [e.g. Updated BPC-5MG price / Added SEMAX-30ML / No changes]

Thanks, [Your Name]
