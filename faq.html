FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How the comparison catalog works, what the listed pricing means, and what to confirm before visiting a third-party vendor.

What is MyPeptidePrice.com?

MyPeptidePrice.com is an independent affiliate price comparison website. We organize vendor listings into comparable product cards so visitors can review prices, sizes, stock status, and known SAMMYC discounts before visiting a third-party vendor.

Does MyPeptidePrice.com sell research products?

No. We do not sell products, process payments, ship orders, or provide customer service for vendor orders. Product links send visitors to independent vendor websites.

How are the comparison cards organized?

Each card represents one product family and one format, such as BPC-157 vials or BPC-157 capsules. Exact sizes are separated into listing buttons. Vendor rows for the selected listing are sorted from lowest estimated after-code price to highest.

How often are prices refreshed?

The catalog is designed to refresh vendor feeds automatically every 15 minutes. If a vendor feed temporarily fails, the site retains the last successful vendor snapshot or a bundled fallback so the comparison page remains usable.

What does the SAMMYC price mean?

When a tracked vendor supports the SAMMYC code, the comparison row estimates the price after the known vendor discount. Confirm that the discount applies and review the final total on the vendor website before completing an order.

What is the 7/7 testing standard?

The standard refers to seven testing categories: Net Purity, Net Content, Heavy Metals, Endotoxins, Identification, Sterility, and Conformity. Review current certificates and product documentation directly with each vendor.

Are affiliate links used?

Yes. Some outbound vendor links are affiliate links. MyPeptidePrice.com may receive compensation when a visitor uses one of those links or codes. This does not change the listed vendor price.

How do I report an incorrect price?

Email contact@mypeptideprice.com with the product, vendor, and listing that appears incorrect. Final prices can change between feed refreshes or during checkout.
