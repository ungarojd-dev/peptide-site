Privacy Policy

How MyPeptidePrice.com collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

MyPeptidePrice.com is an independent research peptide price comparison and information tool. We do not sell products. All purchases made through links on this site occur directly between you and the vendor.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, the following third-party tools are active on this site and may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4, collects anonymized usage data including pages visited, time on site, device type, and approximate location. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Meta Pixel, collects data used to measure ad performance and build advertising audiences. This pixel fires on all pages. Meta Privacy Policy →

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any personally identifying information through this website.

Cookies

This site uses cookies through Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel. These are analytics and advertising cookies used to understand site traffic and ad performance. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites beyond standard analytics and advertising measurement.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Affiliate Links

Links to vendor websites on this site contain affiliate referral parameters (e.g. ?ref=SammyC). When you click these links, the vendor may set a cookie on your browser attributing your visit to us. We earn a commission if you make a purchase. The vendor's own privacy policy governs any data they collect.

Third-Party Websites

This site links to external vendor websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before making any purchase.

Data Retention

We do not store your personal data on our servers. Analytics data is retained by Google and Meta according to their standard retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, correct, or delete data held about you. For data collected by Google Analytics, visit your Google Account settings. For data collected by Meta, visit your Facebook settings.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
