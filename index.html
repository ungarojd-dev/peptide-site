How we compare peptide prices

MyPeptidePrice.com is an independent price reference. We do not sell products or process orders. We track listings from research peptide vendors and normalize them so the same compound and size can be compared side by side.

Where the prices come from

Vendor pricing is pulled directly from each vendor's own store data, then refreshed on a schedule. Each listing shows the vendor's regular price and the estimated total after any known discount code.

How cost per mg is calculated

For compounds measured in milligrams, we divide the estimated after-code price by the listing's total milligrams. This surfaces the true cost per mg, which is often a more useful comparison than the sticker price alone.

How vendors are ordered

Within each compound, vendor rows are sorted from lowest estimated after-code price to highest for the selected size. We do not accept payment for ranking position. Order reflects price, not sponsorship.

Affiliate disclosure

Some outbound links are affiliate links, which may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not affect pricing, ordering, or the position a vendor appears in. See our FAQ for details.
